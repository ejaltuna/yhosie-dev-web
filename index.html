MY BIOGRAPHY

Be a native from “Camaguan” the birthplace of Venezuela folk music, I commenced playing the harp in the footsteps of my father Rafael Rojas a well known artist and part of Juan Vicente Torrealba’s époque which served me as an inspiration to impreted the versions from the “Llano” a unique area near the Amazon basin with nature, flora and fauna.

Happy Clients With my move to Caracas I formed an all female band called “Grupo show Diademas” posed of 2 harps, guitar, base, maracas and vocal; we performed in shows, private functions, clubs, theaters, TV and Miss Venezuela events.

Projects In 2000 I commenced to perform with my sister as a duo harp at the new 5 star Embassy Suites by Hilton Hotel in Caracas with a daily program featuring Venezuelan and International music which until today we host now for 10 years (Frank D’Lis replaced my sister in 2005). During this period we recorded 4 CD’s and performed in private parties, official events by foreign embassy’s, radio and TV.

Years of experience In 2008 I recorded under the supervision of Juan Vicente Torrealba who is considered the “father of Venezuelan music” and has been my idol to follow and he always was part of the family coming from the same town. This recording is taking his music to the highest level with a CD performing as solist and accompanied by string Orchestra, arrangements made by his son Juan Carlos Torrealba and directed by Henry Martinez. Inspired by my surroundings and success I commenced to compose and presently have planned to record my work on a new CD named WITH “Yhosayra Style and Fusion”
